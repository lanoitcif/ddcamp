# D&D 5e: Heroes of Oakhaven
Simplified Character Sheets for the *Dragon of Whispering Peak* campaign.

---

## 1. Lily the Silent
**Player:** 8yo Daughter | **Class:** Thief (Rogue) | **Level:** 1

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 8 (-1) | 16 (+3) | 13 (+1) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) |

- **HP:** 9
- **AC:** 14 (Leather Armor)
- **Initiative:** +3
- **Speed:** 30 ft

**Skills:**
- **Stealth:** +7 (Amazing at hiding and being quiet!)
- **Sleight of Hand:** +5 (Great at picking locks or grabbing Sun-Cakes)
- **Acrobatics:** +5
- **Investigation:** +4

**Actions:**
- **Shortsword:** Roll d20+5 to hit. Damage: **1d6 + 3** piercing.
- **Shortbow:** Roll d20+5 to hit. Damage: **1d6 + 3** piercing (Range 80/320 ft).

**How to Play Lily:**
- **Sneak Attack (+1d6):** Once per turn, you can deal an extra **1d6** damage if you have "Advantage" (rolling two dice and taking the best) or if one of your friends is standing next to the monster you are attacking.
- **Expertise:** You are naturally gifted at being sneaky. Your Stealth bonus is higher than anyone else's!
- **Thieves' Cant:** You can spot secret marks left by other rogues that tell you where treasure might be hidden.

## 2. Brave Thorne
**Player:** 11yo Son | **Class:** Fighter | **Level:** 1

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 16 (+3) | 12 (+1) | 15 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 13 (+1) | 8 (-1) |

- **HP:** 12
- **AC:** 16 (Chain Mail)
- **Initiative:** +1
- **Speed:** 30 ft

**Skills:**
- **Athletics:** +5 (Great at climbing Whispering Peak)
- **Perception:** +3
- **Survival:** +3
- **Animal Handling:** +3

**Actions:**
- **Longsword:** Roll d20+5 to hit. Damage: **1d8 + 3** slashing (or **1d10 + 3** if using two hands).
- **Handaxe:** Roll d20+5 to hit. Damage: **1d6 + 3** slashing (Can be thrown).

**How to Play Thorne:**
- **Second Wind:** Once per battle, you can use a "Bonus Action" to catch your breath. Roll **1d10 + 1** and heal that many Hit Points!
- **Fighting Style:** You are a master of the sword. If you use your Longsword with two hands and roll a 1 or 2 on the damage die, you can roll it again to try and get a better number.
- **High AC:** Your heavy armor makes you very hard to hit. You are the shield of the group!

## 3. Valerius the Just
**Player:** 13yo | **Class:** Paladin | **Level:** 1

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 15 (+2) | 10 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 8 (-1) | 12 (+1) | 14 (+2) |

- **HP:** 12
- **AC:** 18 (Chain Mail & Shield)
- **Initiative:** +0
- **Speed:** 30 ft

**Skills:**
- **Persuasion:** +4 (Useful for talking to dragons!)
- **Insight:** +3 (Tells you if someone is lying)
- **Religion:** +1
- **Athletics:** +4

**Actions:**
- **Warhammer:** Roll d20+4 to hit. Damage: **1d8 + 2** bludgeoning.
- **Javelin:** Roll d20+4 to hit. Damage: **1d6 + 2** piercing (Range 30/120 ft).

**The Oath of Oakhaven:**
*Valerius is sworn to protect the village and its people. His code is: "Protect the weak, uphold the truth, and always offer mercy—even to a lonely dragon."*

**How to Play Valerius:**
- **Lay on Hands:** You have a pool of **5** healing points. You can touch a friend and give them any number of these points to heal their "bonks" or tiredness.
- **Divine Sense:** You can close your eyes and feel if there is any great magic, monsters, or very good beings nearby.
- **Divine Smite (Special):** *[Simplified for Level 1]* Once per long rest, when you hit with your hammer, you can call down golden light to deal an extra **2d8** radiant damage! Useful for when justice needs to be swift.
