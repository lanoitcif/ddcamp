# The Dragon of Whispering Peak
A D&D Adventure for Young Heroes (Ages 8-13)

**Tone:** Classic High Fantasy (Whimsical, Heroic, Kind-hearted)
**Setting:** The peaceful village of Oakhaven and the nearby Whispering Peak.

---

## 🎵 Atmosphere & Vibe
This campaign uses a **Minecraft-style** procedural audio engine to create a cozy, atmospheric experience.
- **Bakery:** Warm, golden pads.
- **Woods:** Ethereal, emerald-tinted melodies.
- **Peak:** Singing wind and sparse, magical bells.

---

## Act 1: The Missing Sun-Cakes
The adventure begins on the morning of the Summer Festival. 

### Interactive Tools:
- **Intro Intro:** DM triggers Mrs. Crumb's opening dialogue on the TV.
- **Spotlight Search:** DM drags the spotlight to find the **Blue Sparkly Scale** and **Golden Crumb Trail**.
- **Handout:** DM shares the "Sun-Cakes" image when players find the empty box.

---

## Act 2: The Sparkle Woods
The trees glow like emeralds and whisper secrets.

### Encounter 1: Hoot the Riddle-Owl
- **The Riddle:** *"I have a heart that doesn't beat, I have a bed but never sleep, I can run but have no legs. What am I?"*
- **Answer:** A River.
- **VTT Tool:** DM uses the typewriter effect to reveal the riddle line-by-line.

### Encounter 2: The Glimmer Stream
- **Interactive:** Players click the stepping stones on the TV to jump across.
- **VTT Tool:** DM monitors "Splashes" and can pulse a **Ping** if a kid is pointing at a specific stone.

---

## Act 3: Whispering Peak
A tall mountain where the wind sounds like soft singing.

### Encounter: Glint the Dragon
Glint is a Small-to-Medium Blue Dragon, currently trying to lick frosting off his claws.
- **Reaction Bar:** DM can use the **❤️ Reaction** or **🌟 Reaction** to reward players who try to make friends with Glint instead of fighting him.
- **Heroic Action:** If a kid is "Bonked" (0 HP), they can use the **Snack** button to recover and keep the friendship talk going!

---

## Conclusion: "The Dragon & The Cake"
Mrs. Crumb renames her bakery in honor of their new friend.

**Final Rewards:**
- **Dragon-Scale Medal:** Shared as a digital handout.
- **Free Sun-Cake Pass:** Shared as a digital handout.
- **🎉 Party Reaction:** A celebratory emoji explosion on the TV!
