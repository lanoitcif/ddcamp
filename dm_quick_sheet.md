# 🐉 DM Quick Sheet: Dragon of Whispering Peak
*Welcome, Dungeon Master! Use this sheet to master the DM Console while you tell your story.*

---

## 🎲 The Console Tools

### 🎛️ Ambience & Audio
- **Start/Playing:** Toggle the procedural audio engine. Each scene has a unique ambient soundtrack.
- **Moods:** `calm` (exploration), `tense` (investigating), `combat` (action). Click to switch — the music crossfades automatically.
- **Volume:** Use the slider to keep it atmospheric but not distracting.

### 🎭 Reactions & Pings
- **Quick Reactions:** Click an emoji (🎉, ❤️, 🌟, ❓, 💀, 🔥, 👏, 😂) to send it floating across the TV. Great for rewarding creativity.
- **Digital Ping:** Click anywhere on the **Scene Context** parchment to pulse a golden ring on the TV.

### ⚔️ HP & Turn Control
- **Initiative:** Click **Next Turn** to cycle the active character. The TV shows a "YOUR TURN" banner.
- **HP Delta:** Type a number (like `-5` or `+2`) and hit **Apply**. Clamped to 0–max.
- **HP +/- Buttons:** Quick single-point adjustments.
- **Heroic Actions:** **Help** logs Advantage, **Snack** heals 2 HP.

### 📜 Handouts
- Click any item in the **Handouts** gallery to show it full-screen on the TV. Click **Dismiss Overlay** to clear.

### 🔦 Puzzle Management
1. Navigate to scene using the **Scenes** sidebar.
2. Click **Launch Puzzle** in the purple Puzzle Controls section.
3. Use DM controls to guide the puzzle.
4. Click **End Puzzle** when done.

### 🗑️ Overlay & Reset
- **Dismiss Overlay:** Clears any active dice roll, toast, or handout overlay on the TV.
- **Reset Campaign:** Double-confirm to wipe all game state and start fresh.

## 🗺️ Scene-by-Scene Reference (12 Scenes)

### Chapter 1: Oakhaven Village

#### 🍞 Bakery — Mrs. Crumb's Bakery
- **Hook:** Sun-Cakes stolen before the Summer Festival!
- **Puzzle:** `Search for Clues` — Drag spotlight to find Blue Scale, Golden Crumbs, Footprint
- **Monsters:** None
- **Quests:** Find the Bakery Clue (Main)

#### 🏪 Market — Oakhaven Market Square
- **Hook:** Barnaby the merchant was also raided. Festival prep is chaotic.
- **Puzzle:** `Ingredient Hunt` — Sort 4 ingredients into 2 recipe bowls
- **Monsters:** None
- **Side Quests:** Deliver Merchant's Parcel, Find Whiskers the Kitten (DC 10), Mrs. Crumb's Secret Recipe (multi-scene)

### Chapter 2: The Sparkle Woods

#### 🌲 Woods — The Sparkle Woods
- **Hook:** Hoot blocks the path with a riddle. Silverfang prowls nearby.
- **Puzzle:** `Hoot's Riddle` — Reveal More / Hints / Answer: "A River"
- **Monsters:** Hoot the Owl (20 HP), Silverfang the Wolf (14 HP)
- **Quests:** Solve Hoot's Riddle (Main), Tame Silverfang (Side — offer food!)

#### 🧚 Glade — The Fairy Glade
- **Hook:** Firefly lanterns escaped! Twinkle the Pixie is mischievous.
- **Puzzle:** `Firefly Catch` — Release fireflies, players catch before they fade
- **Monsters:** Twinkle the Pixie Queen (12 HP), Sporecap the Sprite (10 HP)
- **Side Quests:** Catch Escaped Fireflies, Follow Mushroom Trail
- **Secret Recipe:** Collect Moonberries here!

#### 💧 Stream — The Glimmer Stream
- **Hook:** Liquid silver stream with crystal stepping stones.
- **Puzzle:** `Stepping Stones` — Players pick safe stones across 4 rows
- **Monsters:** None
- **Quest:** Cross the Glimmer Stream (Main)

#### ⛺ Goblin Camp — Goblin Hollow
- **Hook:** Goblins are hungry and scared, not evil. Grumbletooth has a stolen Sun-Cake!
- **Puzzle:** `Sneak Path` — Pick safe routes through 4 checkpoints
- **Monsters:** Grumbletooth (25 HP), Snick the Scout (8 HP)
- **Side Quests:** Negotiate Goblin Truce (diplomacy!), Merchant's Parcel is HERE
- **Key Moment:** Diplomacy vs combat choice. Reward kindness with ❤️ reactions!

### Chapter 3: The Mountain Path

#### 💎 Caves — The Crystal Caverns
- **Hook:** Musical crystals, flutter of crystal bats.
- **Puzzle:** `Crystal Melody` — Simon Says with 4 colored crystals
- **Monsters:** Crystal Bat Swarm (18 HP) — attacks on failed melody
- **Side Quests:** Learn Crystal Melody, Find Lost Traveler (old Finnegan)
- **Secret Recipe:** Collect Cave Crystal here!

#### 🌉 Bridge — The Skythread Bridge
- **Hook:** Rope bridge over a chasm. Shadow wisps below.
- **Monsters:** Shadow Wisp (15 HP) — lonely ghost, can be comforted or fought
- **Side Quest:** Repair Skythread Bridge (3 Athletics checks DC 10)
- **No puzzle** for this scene.

#### 🏕️ Camp — Moonlit Campfire
- **Hook:** Safe resting spot. Heal up, tell stories, stargaze!
- **Puzzle:** `Star Connect` — Connect 7 stars to form a dragon constellation
- **Side Quests:** Share a Hero's Tale (RP), Name a Constellation
- **Secret Recipe:** Collect Star Honey here!
- **HEAL EVERYONE** to full HP here. This is the rest scene.

### Chapter 4: The Summit

#### 🏛️ Ruins — The Forgotten Shrine
- **Hook:** Ancient pillars, cracked altar, glowing runes.
- **Puzzle:** `Rune Match` — Memory game, match 4 pairs of runes
- **Monsters:** The Stone Guardian (35 HP) — MINI-BOSS. Tough fight!
- **Quest:** Restore the Forgotten Shrine (Main) → Ancient Blessing (+1 next roll)
- **Tip:** If heroes solve runes BEFORE combat, Guardian becomes friendly.

#### 🐉 Peak — Whispering Peak
- **Hook:** Glint is lonely, not evil. He's licking frosting off his claws.
- **Puzzle:** `Dragon's Hoard` — Sort 6 treasures into 3 category piles
- **Monsters:** Glint the Dragon (45 HP) — FINAL ENCOUNTER
- **Quest:** Make a New Friend (Main) → Dragon-Scale Medal
- **Two paths:** Friendship (share food, talk) or Combat (he surrenders at 15 HP and cries)

#### 🎉 Celebration — Festival of Heroes
- **Hook:** Oakhaven celebrates! Glint wears a tiny chef hat!
- **No puzzle, no monsters.** Pure celebration.
- **Rewards:** Hand out all earned quest rewards. Share Dragon-Scale Medal handout.
- **Fire off 🎉 reactions!**

## ⏱️ Pacing Guide

| Session Length | Scenes | Style |
|:---:|---|---|
| **1 hour** | bakery → woods → peak → celebration | Skip sides, fast RP |
| **2 hours** | bakery → market → woods → stream → goblin_camp → peak → celebration | Some sides |
| **3 hours** | All 12 scenes in order | All sides, full RP |
| **4+ hours** | All 12 + extended RP, every side quest | Marathon session |

## 🧒 DM Tips for Kids
- **Rule of Cool:** Wild idea? Click ❤️ and say "Roll with Advantage!"
- **Everything is a d20:** 10+ usually works for any creative idea.
- **Handouts on the big screen** make discoveries feel real.
- **Second Wind & Lay on Hands:** Not automated — roll manually and use ±HP.
- **Diplomacy rewards:** Use 🌟 reactions when kids choose kindness over combat.
- **Rest at Camp:** Always heal to full HP at the campfire scene!
- **Side quests are optional:** The DM decides when to introduce them based on pacing and engagement.
