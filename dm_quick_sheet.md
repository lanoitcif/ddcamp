# 🐉 DM Quick Sheet: Dragon of Whispering Peak
*Welcome, Dungeon Master! Use this sheet to master the DM Console v2.0 while you tell your story.*

---

## 🎲 The Console Tools

### 🎛️ Ambience & Audio
- **Start/Playing:** Toggle the Minecraft-style procedural audio engine.
- **Moods:** `calm` (exploration), `tense` (investigating), `combat` (action). Click to switch — the music crossfades automatically.
- **Volume:** Use the slider to keep it atmospheric but not distracting.

### 🎭 Reactions & Pings
- **Quick Reactions:** Click an emoji (🎉, ❤️, 🌟, ❓, 💀, 🔥, 👏, 😂) to send it floating across the TV. Great for rewarding "Rule of Cool" ideas from the kids.
- **Digital Ping:** Click anywhere on the **Scene Context** parchment (middle of the screen) to pulse a golden ring on the TV view.

### ⚔️ HP & Turn Control
- **Initiative:** Click **Next Turn** to cycle who is "Active." The TV will show a banner for that player.
- **HP Delta:** Type a number (like `-5` or `+2`) into the character's HP box and hit **Apply**. The value is added to current HP and clamped to 0–max.
- **HP +/- Buttons:** Quick single-point adjustments.
- **Heroic Actions:** Use the **Help** button to log Advantage, or **Snack** to heal a kid for 2 HP.

### 📜 Handouts
- Click any item in the **Handouts** gallery to show a full-screen image on the TV with a sparkle effect. Click **Dismiss Overlay** to clear it.

### 🔦 Puzzle Management
Each scene has a unique interactive puzzle. To start one:
1. Navigate to the scene using the **Scenes** sidebar.
2. Click **Launch Puzzle** in the purple Puzzle Controls section.
3. Use the DM controls to guide the puzzle (each type is different).
4. Click **End Puzzle** when done.

**Puzzle types by scene:**
1. **Bakery:** `Search for Clues` — A spotlight puzzle. Move the spotlight in the DM box to find clues, then click the clue buttons to reveal them on the TV.
2. **Woods:** `Hoot's Riddle` — A riddle reveal. Click **Reveal More** to show the next clue line on the TV, or **Show All** to reveal everything. Then click the answer button.
3. **Peak:** `Glimmer Stream` — Players see stepping stones on the TV and must pick the right path across.

### 🗑️ Overlay & Reset
- **Dismiss Overlay:** Clears any active dice roll, toast, or handout overlay on the TV.
- **Reset Campaign:** Double-confirm to wipe all game state and start fresh.

## 🌟 Narrative Reference

### Act 1: Mrs. Crumb's Bakery
- **The Hook:** Mrs. Crumb lost her Sun-Cakes! 
- **The Clues:** Blue scales (Glint) and golden crumbs leading to the woods.
- **Intro Intro:** Click the button in the Narration box to pre-fill her dramatic opening lines.

### Act 2: Sparkle Woods
- **Hoot the Owl:** Wise but grumpy. Needs the "River" riddle answer.
- **Glimmer Stream:** Liquid silver. Requires jumping or clever magic to cross.

### Act 3: Whispering Peak
- **Glint the Dragon:** He's not mean, just a lonely teenager with a sweet tooth!
- **Resolution:** Can they convince Glint to be Oakhaven's guest of honor?

## 🧒 DM Tips for Kids
- **The Rule of Cool:** If a kid wants to share their real-life snack with Glint, click the **❤️ Reaction** and say "He loves it! Roll with Advantage!"
- **Everything is a d20:** If they have a wild idea, just ask for a d20 roll. 10+ usually works!
- **Handouts:** When they find something, click the item in the **Handouts** section to show them exactly what it looks like on the big screen.
- **Second Wind & Lay on Hands:** These special abilities aren't automated — roll or track them yourself and use the ±HP input to apply the healing.
